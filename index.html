The Coach

Mark Anthony Rowan Kotze

Born and raised in the historic city of Kimberley, Northern Cape, Mark Kotze is a distinguished public speaker, entrepreneurial mentor, and personal mastery expert. What This Book Is About

The Story Behind the Silence.

This is not a sanitised success story. It is a memoir told with the kind of honesty that most people never dare to speak aloud — because Mark believes your story, no matter how painful, carries the power to set you free.

Radical HonestyMark holds nothing back — the failures, the shame, the turning points. All of it on the page.
Surviving the TrialsA firsthand account of navigating some of life's most extreme personal and professional ordeals.
The Moment It ShiftedThe pivotal decisions and encounters that changed the trajectory of Mark's life completely.
The Right to SpeakWhy your story matters — and why staying silent about your struggle costs more than it protects.
Identity Under PressureHow who you think you are gets tested — and what you discover about yourself when it does.
Choosing a Different PathThe practical and emotional work of deciding to live differently, even when it is the harder road.
The Reader's Journey

Read It. Feel It. Own It.

Arrested is not just a book you read — it is a book you sit with. It will challenge you, move you, and ultimately remind you that no chapter of your life has the power to define your final story.

01 · EncounterMeet Mark's story without filters — raw, real, and unedited.

04 · ReclaimWalk away with permission to rewrite what happens next.
